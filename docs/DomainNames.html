Domain Names and ICANN

The internet corporation for Assigned Names and Numbers, ICANN for short. ICANN is responsible for keeping track of and managing DNS, Domain Name System. Along with it they are responsible for coordinating and ensuring IP addresses on machines are different and unique. Since IP addresses are used to separate one PC from another in the world, this is a very important task. As an organization they were first formed in 1998. Their primary responsibility is to keep the internet secure and stable for everyone. Domain names are titles that follow the name of the website, names such as .org, .net, .com are the most common. These domain names are often how DNS split up the load of billions of requests on a daily basis.
